The Surface That Works Hardest

Your worktop takes more punishment than any other surface in your home. If it's scratched, scorched, or just looks dated, replacing it delivers instant impact. We supply and fit a full range of worktop materials to suit any budget and style.

Worktop Options

From budget-friendly to premium — find your perfect surface

Laminate

  • Most affordable option
  • Huge range of colours and patterns
  • Wood, marble, stone, and concrete effects
  • Durable and easy to maintain

Ideal for: budget-conscious transformations

Solid Wood

  • Natural warmth and character
  • Oak, walnut, beech options
  • Can be sanded and refinished over time
  • Requires occasional oiling

Ideal for: traditional and country kitchens

Quartz Composite

  • Engineered stone, extremely durable
  • Heat and scratch resistant
  • Non-porous, hygienic
  • Wide range of colours

Ideal for: premium, low-maintenance kitchens

Solid Surface (Corian-style)

  • Seamless joins possible
  • Integrated sinks available
  • Can be repaired if scratched
  • Smooth, consistent appearance

Ideal for: contemporary, minimalist kitchens
