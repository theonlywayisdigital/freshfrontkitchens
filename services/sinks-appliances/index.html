Sinks & Appliances

Complete your kitchen transformation with quality fixtures and appliances that work seamlessly with your new look.

The Finishing Touches

While refreshing your doors and worktops makes the biggest visual impact, many customers choose to update their sink, taps, or appliances at the same time. Because we're already working in your kitchen, adding these items is straightforward and cost-effective—and everything is installed in one visit.

Kitchen Sinks

A new sink can completely change your kitchen's character. We supply and install sinks to suit every style and budget.

Stainless Steel

The practical classic. Durable, hygienic, and available in various configurations from single bowl to 1.5 bowl with drainer.

Composite / Granite

Modern composite sinks in black, grey, or white make a statement. Extremely durable and resistant to scratches and heat.

Belfast / Butler

The farmhouse favourite. Deep ceramic sinks with that classic apron front—perfect for traditional and country kitchens.

Undermount

Mounted beneath stone or solid surface worktops for a seamless look. Easy to wipe crumbs straight into the sink.

Kitchen Taps

From simple mixers to boiling water taps, we supply taps to match your style and needs.

Mixer Taps

Single lever or twin handle designs in chrome, brushed steel, or on-trend black and brass finishes.

Boiling Water Taps

Instant 100°C water at the touch of a button. Say goodbye to waiting for the kettle—ideal for busy households.

Bridge Taps

Traditional two-handle design with exposed pipework. Perfect for heritage and farmhouse kitchen styles.

Pull-Out Spray

Flexible hose taps with spray function. Brilliant for rinsing dishes, filling pans, and cleaning the sink itself.

Kitchen Appliances

While appliance selection is vast, we can supply and fit integrated and freestanding appliances as part of your kitchen refresh.

Built-in Ovens

Single, double, and combination ovens from leading brands.

Hobs & Cooktops

Gas, ceramic, and induction options to suit your cooking style.

Dishwashers

Integrated and slimline models that hide behind your new door fronts.

Fridges & Freezers

Integrated units that seamlessly blend with your cabinetry.

Extractors & Hoods

Chimney, integrated, and downdraft extraction for a fresh kitchen.

Washing Machines

Integrated washers and washer-dryers hidden behind door panels.

A Note on Cabinet Housing

Since we keep your existing cabinet carcasses, any new integrated appliances need to fit the same apertures. During your consultation, we'll measure everything and advise on compatible sizes. If housing adjustments are needed, we'll include that in your quote—no surprises.
