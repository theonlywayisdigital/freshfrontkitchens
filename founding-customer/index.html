Common Questions

How much will I actually save?

Every kitchen is different, but founding customers typically save 40-60% compared to our standard pricing. You're paying what we pay — nothing more.

Is the quality really the same?

Absolutely. These kitchens are going in our portfolio — we want them to look incredible! You'll receive the same materials, craftsmanship, and attention to detail as any customer.

What if I'm not comfortable with photos/video?

We understand. Photos of your kitchen are essential, but we're flexible on showing faces. We can discuss what you're comfortable with during the consultation.

How many founding customers are you accepting?

We're limiting founding customer positions to ensure we can deliver exceptional results and service. Once we've filled our spots, this offer ends.

What areas are eligible?

We're looking for founding customers in Chester, Wirral, Cheshire, North Wales, and Warrington. If you're nearby but not sure, apply anyway!
