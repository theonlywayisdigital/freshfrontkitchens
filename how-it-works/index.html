Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a kitchen makeover take?

Most kitchens are completed in 2-3 days. Larger or more complex projects may take a little longer, but we'll always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Will my kitchen still be usable during the work?

We work as efficiently as possible to minimise disruption. You may have limited access to certain areas during installation, but we'll work with you to make it as easy as possible.

What if my cabinets are damaged?

During our consultation, we'll assess the condition of your cabinets. Minor repairs can often be made. If cabinets need replacing, we can advise on the best approach.

Can I change the layout of my kitchen?

Kitchen makeovers work with your existing layout. If you want to change the layout, that's a bigger project — but we can discuss options during your consultation.

What warranty do you offer?

All our work comes with a warranty for your peace of mind. We'll explain the full details during your quote.
